About Us



Sunny Corner Cafe

At Sunny Corner Cafe, we specialize in creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can enjoy the finest coffee, delicious pastries, and hearty meals. Located in the heart of Kingston, Jamaica, we pride ourselves on offering exceptional service, fostering great conversations, and delivering unforgettable experiences.



Our Story

Sunny Corner Cafe began as a dream shared by five friends who wanted to create a space where people could come together to relax, connect, and enjoy great food and coffee. What started as a small idea has grown into a beloved community hub, where every cup of coffee tells a story of passion and dedication.



Our Vision

Our vision is to become the number one café destination in Jamaica, known for our exceptional coffee, welcoming atmosphere, and commitment to quality. We aim to inspire a sense of community and create a space where everyone feels at home.
